LIVE
BEYOND
LIFE

they had a plan for you.

degree. job. family. repeat.

a life already written before you had anything to say about it. clean. predictable. safe. and if you deviated. if you picked up a pen, opened a canvas, chased something that lived in your chest instead of on a checklist. they had a word for that too.

waste.

360SPRNG exists because some of us heard that word and built anyway.

not in spite of the pressure. through it. underneath it. in the hours nobody sanctioned. with tools nobody handed us. toward something nobody could see yet but us.

this is not a brand for everyone. it was never meant to be.

it's for the ones who felt the weight of a life prescribed by someone else and chose the harder, realer thing. the ones who understand that there is a version of you that dies quietly if you let it.

the frat is everyone who made the same refusal.

not a following. not an audience. a body of people who understood the cost of choosing their own life and paid it anyway.

if you found this, something in you already recognized it.

live beyond life.

beyond the life they handed you. beyond the version of you they were comfortable with. beyond the quiet death of becoming what someone else needed you to be.
